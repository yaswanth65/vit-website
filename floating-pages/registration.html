Online Course Registration Process

Welcome, students! This guide will walk you through the online course registration process. Please follow these steps carefully to ensure successful registration.

Step 1: Accessing the Registration Portal

  • You will receive an email containing a registration link along with the schedule.
  • Click on the provided link to access the registration portal.
  • Carefully read the instructions displayed on the screen.
  • Important: Do not refresh or open multiple tabs to avoid errors.
  • Click "Agree" to proceed to the login page.

Step 2: Logging In

  • Enter your registration number and password.
  • Click on "Login" to enter the registration module.

Step 3: Understanding Credit Limits

  • Minimum credits per semester: 16
  • Maximum credits per semester: 27
  • The credit counter will update as you register for courses.

Step 4: Registering for Courses

Soft Skills Course (Mandatory)

  • The first page will display the Soft Skills course, which is compulsory.
  • Click "Register" to enroll.
  • Choose a faculty and a time slot.
  • Click "Regular" and then "Register".
  • A confirmation message will appear.

Viewing Registered Courses

  • Click on "View Registered Courses" to see course details and timetable.

Step 5: Selecting Additional Courses

University Core Courses

  • Navigate to "University Core".
  • Select a subject from the available options (e.g., English, Environmental Studies).
  • Choose a faculty and available time slot.
  • Click "Regular" and then "Register".
  • If the selected slot is unavailable, choose a different slot.

Program Core Courses

  • Navigate to "Program Core".
  • Choose a required subject (e.g., Data Structures, Digital Logic Design).
  • Select a faculty and available time slot.
  • Click "Regular" and then "Register".
  • Ensure no time slot conflicts.

Elective Courses

  • Navigate to "Elective Courses".
  • Choose from available program electives and university electives.
  • Follow the same registration procedure as above.

Step 6: Handling Slot Conflicts

  • If an error message appears due to a slot clash, check your View Registered Courses.
  • Identify free slots and modify existing registrations accordingly.
  • Modify time slots if needed before registering for a new course.

Step 7: Finalizing Registration

  • Recommended credit range: 21 to 23 credits.
  • Check your total registered credits.
  • Ensure you meet all program requirements.
  • Review your registered courses in View Registered Courses.

Step 8: Completing the Registration Process

  • After registering all required courses, verify that all courses are correctly listed.
  • Log out of the system once you confirm your registration.
  • Check your email regularly for further instructions or changes.

Important Notes

  • Pass/Fail Courses: Some courses like Environmental Studies are mandatory but do not add credits.
  • Backlog Courses: You may re-register for failed courses if results are declared.
  • Foreign Language: If required, ensure to register for it in an available time slot.

By following these steps, you can successfully complete your course registration. Happy learning!
