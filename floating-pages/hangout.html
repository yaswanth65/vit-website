VIT-AP Student Hangout Guide

Top 10 Cafés & Social Spots

Bhavani Island Adventure

Located in the middle of Krishna River | Perfect for boating, swimming, and adventure activities | Popular among college students for group outings.

Prakasam Barrage Scenic

Beautiful bridge over Krishna River | Magnificently illuminated in the evening | Great spot for evening walks and photography.

Brew Buzz Café

Located on Babu Textiles Road | Popular café with good ambiance for student meetups.

Verandah Coffee Roasters and Café Café

Trendy café in Guru Nanak Colony | Ideal for coffee and conversations.

ORKA CAFE Café

Modern café in KDGO Colony, Guru Nanak area | Offers good food options.

Midtown Sports Café Sports

Located in Acharya Ranga Nagar | Perfect for sports enthusiasts who want to catch games while hanging out.

Kava Cafe Café

Stylish café opposite Manor Food Plaza in Moghalrajpuram.

Blind Chemistry Café

Located near D V Manor Hotel on Tikkle Road | Offers unique ambiance for student gatherings.

Monk's Kitchen Café

Located in Guru Nanak Colony | Popular spot for casual dining and hangouts.

Trippy Tree Cafe Café

Trendy café in Bharathi Nagar | Open from 11 AM to 9:30 PM.

Historical and Nature Spots

Undavalli Caves Historical

1300-year-old rock-cut temples carved from sandstone | Entrance fee: ₹5 | Budget-friendly for students.

Mogalarajapuram Caves Historical

1500-year-old caves located in the heart of Vijayawada City | Perfect for history enthusiasts with limited time.

Gandhi Hill Scenic

Features a 52-feet stupa, library, planetarium, and toy train | Panoramic city views | Excellent for sunset watching with friends.

Kondapalli Fort Historical

About 24 kilometers from Vijayawada | Perfect for a day trip with friends interested in exploring historical sites.

Guntur Area Hangout Spots

TEAS N TREES Café

Popular café in Pandaripuram, Guntur | Open from 11 AM to 11 PM.

The Hangout Café

Modern café beside CENTRO FOOTWARE on Lakshmipuram Main Road in Guntur.

The Campbel Lounge Lounge

Stylish lounge in Brodipet, Guntur | Open from 10:30 AM to 10:30 PM.

Flash Kafei Dain Café

24-hour café opposite Wood Land Function Hall in Guntur | Perfect for late-night study sessions or hangouts.

Barista Café

Well-known coffee chain on Mahatma Gandhi Inner Ring Road in Gorantla, Guntur.

Suryalanka Beach Beach

Located 50 kilometers from Guntur | Offers golden sands and clear waters | Ideal for group trips when you have a full day free.
