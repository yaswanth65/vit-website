Hostel Refund Policy

Welcome, students! This guide will walk you through the hostel refund policy. Please follow these steps carefully to ensure successful registration.

First Year Students

Admission Period:

  • Hostel Admission Fee of Rs. 15,000 is non-refundable.
  • Hostel Caution Deposit Fee of Rs. 15,000 is refundable after course completion or within 45 working days for withdrawn candidates.
  • Hostel Room Fee and Mess Fee deductions: 10% per month, no room fee refund after 4 months.

Expulsion/Sickness/Withdrawal:

  • Mess and AC/Electricity charges refunded on pro-rata basis.
  • Caution Deposit refunded after 45 days.

Suspension:

  • No refund of Hostel Fee for the year.
  • Mess refund on pro-rata basis.
  • Caution Deposit refunded after course completion.

Caution Deposit Refund:

  • Refunded after deducting Annual Maintenance Charge (Rs. 300 per year) and Session Charge (Rs. 100 per year).

Laundry Fee:

  • No refund for laundry.

Senior Students (Other than First Year)

Vacating On Their Own:

  • 10% deduction per month for up to 4 months, no room fee refund after 4 months.
  • Caution Deposit refunded with Academic Caution Deposit after course completion.

Expulsion/Sickness/Withdrawal:

  • Mess and AC/Electricity charges refunded on pro-rata basis.
  • Caution Deposit refunded after 45 days.

Suspension:

  • No refund of Hostel Fee for the year.
  • Mess refund on pro-rata basis.
  • Caution Deposit refunded after course completion.

Final Year Students for Project Purpose:

  • Refund on pro-rata basis within 45 days.
  • Re-joining requires payment of hostel fees (excluding admission fee).

NRI/Foreign Students:

  • Must clear previous year dues before current year allotment.
  • Final year students must clear dues before project or fall semester completion.

Caution Deposit Refund:

  • Refunded after deducting Annual Maintenance Charge (Rs. 300 per year) and Session Charge (Rs. 100 per year).

Laundry Fee:

  • No refund for laundry.

Note: Hostel fee includes Room Charges, General Electricity Charges, and AC Electricity Charges (for AC rooms). Refunds will be processed within one month of withdrawal request and document submission.

VIT AP Hostel Administration

Freshers' Hostel Admission Information 2024-25:

  • Students residing within places wherever University transport facility provided will not be permitted to report as hosteler.
  • Hostel accommodation can be availed on payment of tuition fee followed by hostel fee.
  • Hostel fee inclusive of mess and laundry charges can be paid on selection of room and mess type through the student login within the same day.
  • Students are allowed to report with the allotment letter that can be downloaded from the VTOP only after the hostel fee payment.
  • Failed to make the payment before the due date stands cancellation of rooms. So, the student has to book the room again after the due date.
  • Hostel admission fee is NON-REFUNDABLE on withdrawal after allotment of the room regardless of the student joining the hostel or not.
  • Hostel fees will be refunded as per the Hostel Refund Policy.
  • Withdrawal of hostel admission should be made in the format available on the VIT website.
  • Students are allowed to report on specified dates which will be intimated later.
  • Students are allowed to report only between 9 am and 6 pm. Hence, plan your arrival accordingly.
  • Students on joining should open a savings bank account with the Indian Bank, VIT AP Amaravati Branch to facilitate finance office to process transactions. Hence, it is advised to report with all necessary documents related to open the bank account.
  • Students should bring the following items at the time of joining the hostel:
    • Mattress
    • Pillow
    • Bed Sheets
    • Bucket
    • Mug
    • Lock-1 (small size) for the cupboard and lock-2 (Medium size) for the room.
    • For shared accommodation, the room lock should have multiple duplicate keys that should be submitted at the office (kindly paste the room number on the key to be submitted in the office).
    • The said items are also available inside the campus.
  • NOTE: Applicable to students of MEN'S HOSTEL Joint affidavit (https://vitap.ac.in/hostels/) in non-judicial stamp paper of Rs.20/- is to be submitted by the student duly signed by the parent/guardian on the day of reporting to UG Admission Office.
  • NOTE: Applicable to students of LADIES HOSTEL Joint affidavit (https://vitap.ac.in/hostels/) in non-judicial stamp paper of Rs.20/- inclusive of local guardian details only if preferred (not mandatory) is to be submitted by the student duly signed by the parent/guardian on the day of reporting to the respective block supervisor.
